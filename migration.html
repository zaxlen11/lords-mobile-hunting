Migration Guide

Migration Info

  • If your Castle is at Lv 5 and below, you can use a Novice Relocator to migrate to another Kingdom within the first 3 days of account creation.
  • If your Castle is at Lv 6 and above, you must use Migration Scrolls to migrate to another Kingdom.
  • If your target Kingdom is protected (less than 90 days old):
  • You can migrate if the target Kingdom is older than your current Kingdom.
  • If your current Kingdom is older, you will have to wait until the target Kingdom's protection is over.
  • Migration Scrolls required to Migrate will depend on your might ranking in your target Kingdom. If you are ranked 51 or below, you will only need 1 Migration Scroll.
Note: Your Colosseum rank will be reset after migration. You can only bring a certain amount of resources (according to your Vault limit) to another Kingdom. Excess resources will be lost.
